# Content Strategy Validation Checklist

## Foundation
- [ ] Goals are specific and measurable
- [ ] Target audience defined with personas
- [ ] Brand voice documented
- [ ] Resource constraints understood

## Content Pillars
- [ ] 3-5 pillars defined
- [ ] Pillars align with business
- [ ] Pillars address audience needs
- [ ] Subtopics identified per pillar
- [ ] Content mix balanced

## Formats
- [ ] Format choices justified
- [ ] Frequency realistic
- [ ] Resource needs assessed
- [ ] Content tiers defined

## Distribution
- [ ] All channels mapped
- [ ] Owned channels prioritized
- [ ] Earned opportunities identified
- [ ] Paid amplification planned
- [ ] Repurposing framework exists

## Editorial Calendar
- [ ] Publishing cadence realistic
- [ ] Monthly themes planned
- [ ] Production workflow documented
- [ ] Approval process clear
- [ ] Seasonal dates noted

## Measurement
- [ ] KPIs defined
- [ ] Current baselines captured
- [ ] Targets are realistic
- [ ] Review cadence established

## Report Quality
- [ ] Strategy is actionable
- [ ] Resource requirements clear
- [ ] First month plan specific
- [ ] Quick wins identified
