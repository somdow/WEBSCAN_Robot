TOP 5 COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE (WEBSCAN ROBOT)
As of: February 23, 2026 (US)
Purpose: Quick reference for market positioning, services, and pricing model structures.

==================================================
1) SE RANKING
==================================================
Core services:
- All-in-one SEO platform (site audit, rank tracking, backlink tools, on-page checks)
- Agency-focused add-ons (Agency Pack, white-label options, lead generation widget)
- Reporting workflows and API options

Pricing structure:
- Tiered subscription model by plan and usage limits
- Publicly shown tiers include Essential, Pro, Business
- Monthly billing and discounted annual billing
- Add-ons available (for example: Local Marketing, Agency Pack)
- Public pricing snapshot observed: Essential starts at $65/mo (monthly) or $52/mo (annual equivalent)

Sources:
- https://seranking.com/pricing.html
- https://help.seranking.com/hc/en-us/articles/16333590268572-Agency-Pack-Overview

2) SITECHECKER
==================================================

- Monthly and annual billing options

Sources:

POSITIONING TAKEAWAY FOR WEBSCAN ROBOT
==================================================
Market gap to emphasize:
- Most competitors are SEO-first platforms with varying agency/reporting options.
- WebScan Robot should position as a website intelligence platform that combines:
  SEO + technical health + security + trust signals + AI recommendations + white-label client delivery in one workflow.

Messaging angle:
- "Beyond SEO audits" is still valid.
- Accessibility modules can extend this into "website quality intelligence" rather than pure SEO tooling.
