16) BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES
==================================================
Tone:
- Expert but clear
- Practical, not hype-heavy
- Outcome-focused

Language rules:
- Lead with business impact, then technical depth
- Prefer concrete verbs: detect, prioritize, fix, deliver, scale
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable

Avoid:
- “Magic AI” claims
- Vague “best-in-class” filler
- Overpromising rankings or guaranteed outcomes

==================================================
17) CLAIMS SAFETY NOTES
==================================================
Use careful phrasing:
- “helps improve” instead of “guarantees”
- “can reduce risk” instead of “eliminates risk”
- “supports compliance readiness” instead of “certifies compliance”
