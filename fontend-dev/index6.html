Don't just check the homepage — check every page

Your homepage might look great, but what about the other 99 pages? Hello Toolkit auto-discovers and audits up to 100 pages per site, so hidden issues on deep pages never slip through.

Works even on sites protected by Cloudflare, SiteGround, and Sucuri — with automatic browser rendering fallback.

Catch security risks before they become problems

Dedicated security modules audit your headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options), detect mixed HTTP/HTTPS content, probe for exposed sensitive files (.env, .git, wp-config), and verify blacklist status via Google Web Risk API.

Security vulnerabilities hurt your search rankings, erode visitor trust, and put your business at risk. Find them first.

Found an issue? AI helps you fix it instantly

Every issue comes with AI-generated fix suggestions — optimized rewrites, improved meta tags, content recommendations, and executive summaries. Choose from three AI providers: OpenAI GPT-4o, Anthropic Claude, or Google Gemini.

You don't need to be a developer to improve your website. AI turns audit findings into one-click fixes.

One click generates a branded PDF report with your full audit results, category breakdowns, and fix recommendations. Send it to clients, share with your team, or attach it to a proposal.
